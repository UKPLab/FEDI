The Postepay Evolution Card (hereinafter also the "Card") is the electronic money instrument, nominative, prepaid and rechargeable, made available by PostePay S.p.A. that allows the Cardholder, within the limits of the amount available, to make cash withdrawals and payment transactions (with the exception of purchases by mail or telephone and all transactions that are not authorized online i.e. at the same time as the payment) in Italy and abroad. In addition, the Card allows for the payment of highway tolls on the enabled sections whose updated list can be found on Poste Italiane's websites www.poste.it and www.postepay.it. Cash withdrawals can be made at Post Offices and postal and bank ATMs participating in the Mastercard circuit.
